The problem

UW has the data. Students still plan by hand.

MyPlan, DARS, and a 14,000-course catalog all exist — but nothing turns "what are my requirements and prerequisites?" into "what do I take, and when?"

  • Prerequisite chains are a maze — one wrong order can delay graduation a full year.
  • Degree requirements live in dense department PDFs.
  • Sequencing 12+ quarters around prereqs and offerings is manual guesswork.
  • Transfer and returning students feel it most.

The solution

Your major + your courses → a clear plan.

Pick your major and the app builds a prerequisite-valid plan instantly — then you shape it, quarter by quarter.

Plan

An auto-scheduler seeds a quarter-by-quarter plan that respects every prerequisite.

Edit

Browse the catalog and add, remove, or move courses between quarters.

Explore

An interactive map shows how each course flows into the ones it unlocks.

Landscape & market

Different by design — built for every Husky.

What exists today

MyPlanBuilds a schedule — no prereq-valid degree path
DARSA checklist of what's left — no sequencing
prereq-mapVisual only (archived 2022) — no personalization
UsAuto-planned + editable + an interactive prereq map, per major

Who it's for

  • ~36,000 UW Seattle undergrads (~60k across campuses) — all plan a degree.
  • Highest need: freshmen, transfer students, pre-major applicants to capacity-capped majors, and major-switchers.
  • Free, no login — zero-friction; expandable to every major & peer schools.

The impact

Clearer planning, on-time graduation.

  • For students: better course decisions, fewer wasted credits, less adviser back-and-forth.
  • Extensible by design: every major is pure data — a new program is one file, no code.
  • A genuine gap: UW-IT's own prereq-map (archived 2022) only visualized prerequisites; this plans a personalized degree.
A natural input to MyPlan and advising — turning static requirements into a living, personal roadmap for every Husky.
